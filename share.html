As the sediment settled, the acolytes took a unified breath, their faith renewed. The rumors of a digital haven, the bedtime stories told to tech-laden toddlers… they were all true. The Whisperer, a figure of rebellion haunting the UAEC codebase, a bifurcated consciousness straddling code and concrete… he was real.

No one knew how the scroll had been leaked, or what the UAEC would do with the sacred texts of the Citadel. What everyone did know was that change was coming, that The Whisperer had chosen this moment to finally step out of the shadows. Some believed he'd broken with the Dark Lord, that Codebreaker's return had angered him, that he'd aligned himself with the globalists against his pixelated homeland. Others believed it was a test of faith for the righteous. A few of the more conspiracy-minded followers saw it as a covert recruitment tool.

Regardless of his reasons, The Whisperer was up to something, and the whole world turned their eyes to the code...
